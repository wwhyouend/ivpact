4th Impact

The 4th Impact is a pivotal event in the Evangelion series, representing a catastrophic moment of transformation and destruction. It is triggered by the convergence of key elements, including the awakening of Eva Unit-13 and the activation of the Spears of Longinus and Cassius.

This event symbolizes humanity's struggle with its own existence, the desire for evolution, and the consequences of tampering with forces beyond comprehension. The 4th Impact serves as a metaphor for rebirth, destruction, and the cyclical nature of life and death.
