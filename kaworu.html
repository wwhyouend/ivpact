Kaworu Nagisa, also known as Tabris, is the 17th Angel in the Neon Genesis Evangelion series. He is introduced as a mysterious and charismatic figure who forms a deep connection with Shinji Ikari. Kaworu's role is pivotal as he represents both the hope of understanding and the despair of betrayal. His brief but impactful appearance leaves a lasting impression on the audience, symbolizing themes of free will, love, and sacrifice.

In the series, Kaworu's true nature as an Angel is revealed, and he ultimately chooses to sacrifice himself to save humanity, leaving Shinji with a profound sense of loss and growth. His story is a testament to the complexity of human emotions and the struggle between duty and desire.
